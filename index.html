When was the last one we survived?

December 21, 2012

The so-called Mayan apocalypse at the start of the 14th b'ak'tun. The Earth would be destroyed by an asteroid, Nibiru, or some other interplenetary object; an alian invasion; or a supernova. Scientists from NASA, along with expert archeologists stated that none of those events were possible.
